What makes Janapriya Secondary School distinctive?

Diverse Curriculum
Our college is unique for offering a diverse and flexible curriculum that allows students to explore subjects like science, humanities, business, and vocational courses. We provide pathways for students to specialize in areas of their interest, including programs that help students earn college credits early or gain practical experience in their future careers.
Active Learning
We focus on modern teaching methods that encourage active learning. Students engage in project-based work, use technology in the classroom, and receive personalized support from teachers to ensure they understand the material and can apply it in real life.
Inclusive Environment
Our college environment is inclusive and supportive, where every student is treated with respect. We provide extra help for students with different learning needs and promote social-emotional learning to build important life skills like resilience, teamwork, and self-confidence.
Extracurricular Opportunities
Outside of classes, students have the chance to participate in various extracurricular activities like sports, arts, clubs, and leadership opportunities. These activities help students grow their skills and discover their passions beyond academics
